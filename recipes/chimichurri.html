Chimichurri

Description

Delicious sauce on steak and many other things. Nice hit you in the face aroma and distinct taste set chimichurri sauces apart.

The origin of chimichurri sauces is, shit, I don't know. But I should research and add a section.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot ©)
